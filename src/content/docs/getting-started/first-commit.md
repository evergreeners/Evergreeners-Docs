# Your First Commit

Ready to start your journey? Here's how to make your first commit that counts towards your Evergreeners streak.

## Pick a Task

The best way to start is by picking a small task. Check our [Issues](https://github.com/evergreeners/evergreeners/issues) for "good first issue" tags.

## The Workflow

1. **Fork** the repository.
2. **Clone** your fork.
3. **Create a branch** for your change: `git checkout -b my-new-feature`
4. **Make your changes**.
5. **Commit** with a clear message: `git commit -m "Add feature X"`
6. **Push** to your fork: `git push origin my-new-feature`
7. **Open a Pull Request**.

## Seeing the Impact

Once your PR is merged (or even just pushed to your own tracked repo), check your Evergreeners dashboard. You'll see your contribution heatmap light up and your streak begin!
