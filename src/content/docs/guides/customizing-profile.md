# Customizing Your Profile

Your profile is your digital CV and your garden. Here's how to make it stand out.

## Editing Bio & Socials

Go to your **Settings** to add your bio, Twitter link, and personal website. A complete profile earns more trust in the community.

## Garden Themes

As you level up, you'll unlock different themes for your Consistency Garden. These change the colors, plant types, and background effects of your heatmap.

## Showcasing Projects

You can "Pin" specific projects to the top of your profile. These projects will display more detailed stats and a larger visual representation in your garden.

## Badges & Titles

Display your hardest-earned achievements. You can select up to 3 badges to feature next to your username.
