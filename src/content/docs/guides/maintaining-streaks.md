# Maintaining Streaks

A streak is more than a number; it's a habit. Here's how to keep yours alive even when things get tough.

## Smallest Possible Step

On days when you have zero energy, find the "smallest possible version" of your work.
- Fixing a typo in the README.
- Adding a single unit test.
- Refactoring one small function.

The goal is to keep the momentum, not necessarily to build a whole new feature.

## Schedule Your Peak Time

Identify when you are most productive and protect that time. For many, a "Power Hour" in the morning before starting work is the secret to a multi-year streak.

## Using Shields Wisely

Don't wait until you're exhausted to use a **Streak Shield**. If you know a busy weekend is coming, plan to use your earned shields to maintain your sanity while keeping your rank.

## Community Accountability

Share your streak in the [Community](/community) page. Knowing others are watching your garden grow is a powerful motivator.
