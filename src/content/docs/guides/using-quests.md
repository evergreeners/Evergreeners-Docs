# Using Quests

Quests are the best way to earn reputation and discover new features in Evergreeners.

## Finding Quests

1. Navigate to the **Quests** tab in your dashboard.
2. Browse the **Available Quests**.
3. Use filters to find quests that match your favorite languages or skill level.

## Accepting a Quest

Once you find a quest you like, click **Accept**. This move it to your "Active Quests" list. Most quests have a time limit, so make sure you're ready to commit!

## Tracking Progress

The Quest page will show you exactly what steps are remaining. For coding quests, Evergreeners will automatically scan your repositories for matching activity.

## Claiming Rewards

Once all tasks are finished, the quest will move to "Completed." Your XP and rewards will be automatically added to your profile.
