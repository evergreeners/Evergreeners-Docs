# Overview

Evergreeners is not a simple productivity tool but a system designed to track developer consistency over time. It helps developers cultivate their digital garden through daily contributions and community engagement.

## What is Evergreeners?

Evergreeners is a platform that transforms your GitHub contributions into a thriving digital garden. By focusing on consistency rather than just intensity, it encourages sustainable growth and long-term skill development.

## Key Features

- **Contribution Heatmap**: A visual representation of your daily impact.
- **Streak Engine**: Track your consistency and maintain your momentum.
- **Quests system**: Engage with challenges to level up your skills.
- **Reputation mechanics**: Grow your standing in the developer community.
