# Why Evergreeners Exists

## The Consistency Gap

Many developers struggle with the "heroic effort" cycle: starting projects with high enthusiasm only to abandon them when life gets busy. This lack of consistency prevents meaningful mastery and long-term project success.

## Why Consistency Matters

Consistency is the compound interest of self-improvement. By showing up every day, you:
1. **Build Momentum**: It's easier to keep going than to start fresh.
2. **Deepen Neural Pathways**: Regular practice leads to better retention.
3. **Establish Trust**: In open source, consistency is a signal of reliability to collaborators and users.

## How We're Different

Traditional productivity apps focus on "checking off tasks." Evergreeners focuses on **maintenance and growth**. We don't just care that you finished a feature; we care that you're consistently contributing to your craft.

We exist to help you bridge the gap between "I want to be a developer" and "I am a developer who builds things every day."
