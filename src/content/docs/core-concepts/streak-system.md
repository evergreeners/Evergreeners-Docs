# Streak System

The **Streak System** is the core mechanic that keeps you coming back. It tracks how many consecutive days you've performed a meaningful development action.

## What Counts?

Not every commit is equal. To maintain your streak, Evergreeners looks for:
1. **Meaningful Changes**: Repositories with active code changes.
2. **Issue Activity**: Discussing architecture or fixing bugs.
3. **Peer Review**: Reviewing pull requests for others.

## Streak Shield

Life happens. Whether it's a holiday, illness, or just a much-needed break, you can earn **Streak Shields** through consistent performance. A shield allows you to miss a day without resetting your main counter.

## Multipliers

The longer your streak, the higher your reputation multiplier becomes. However, we also implement "Cool Down" periods to ensure that the pursuit of a streak doesn't lead to exhaustion.
