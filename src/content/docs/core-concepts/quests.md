# Quests

**Quests** are time-bound challenges designed to push you out of your comfort zone and introduce you to new parts of the ecosystem.

## Types of Quests

- **Onboarding Quests**: Simple tasks like "Connect your first repo" or "Update your bio."
- **Skill Quests**: Master a specific technology, e.g., "Complete 5 Pull Requests in a TypeScript project."
- **Community Quests**: Collaborative goals, e.g., "Help 3 newcomers with their first issues."
- **Seasonal Quests**: Limited-time events with unique rewards.

## Rewards

Completing quests earns you:
- **Reputation (XP)**: Levels up your profile.
- **Badges**: Show off your achievements.
- **Garden Decals**: Unique items to decorate your Consistency Garden.
