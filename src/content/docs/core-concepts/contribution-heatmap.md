# Contribution Heatmap

The **Contribution Heatmap** is a powerful tool inspired by the GitHub activity chart, but enhanced with deeper metrics and custom themes.

## Beyond Pixels

While GitHub shows *that* you committed, the Evergreeners Heatmap analyzes *what* you achieved:
- **Impact Score**: A weighted metric combining lines of code, commit complexity, and issue resolution.
- **Multilingual Support**: Color-coding by the technology stack used that day.
- **Milestones**: Special markers for days you hit significant goals.

## The "Evergreen" Goal

Your goal is to maintain a consistent shade of green (or your chosen theme color) across the map. Blank spots are natural—rest is part of growth—but the heatmap helps you visualize your patterns over months and years.
