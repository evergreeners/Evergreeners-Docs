# Consistency Garden

The **Consistency Garden** is the central visual metaphor of Evergreeners. It represents your growth as a developer through the lens of a thriving ecosystem.

## How it Works

Every repository you track acts as a specific "plot" in your garden. As you contribute code, you plant and nurture different types of digital flora.

- **Seeds**: New projects or initial commits.
- **Growth**: Consistent daily commits help your plants grow taller and more complex.
- **Maintenance**: Regular updates keep your garden "evergreen" and prevent it from wilting.

## Visual Indicators

Your garden's appearance changes based on:
- **Language Diversity**: Different colors representing different programming languages.
- **Commit Frequency**: The density of the vegetation.
- **Longevity**: Older, consistently maintained projects become large "Legacy Trees."
